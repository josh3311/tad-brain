# Market Scan — 2026-06-09-0422

## #1 — AI Model Output Attribution & Source Tracker (Score: 32/40)

**Problem:** Enterprises cannot trace which specific API call, model version, or provider generated which output in production systems, making debugging, compliance audits, and cost allocation impossible across multi-model deployments

**Scores:** Demand 8 | Competition 8 | Buildability 8 | Revenue Speed 8

**Evidence:** Enterprise AI ops teams on r/MachineLearning report inability to track outputs to source models; compliance officers struggle with audit trails for regulated industries; no existing product provides complete request-to-output lineage tracking

**Why no competition:** Requires real-time instrumentation across multiple AI providers and custom deployment monitoring—too niche for generic observability tools, too infrastructure-specific for pure SaaS solutions

## #2 — Multi-Provider Token Count Standardizer & Budget Enforcer (Score: 31/40)

**Problem:** Different LLM providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Llama) count tokens differently for the same input, causing budget overruns, unpredictable costs, and inability to fairly compare model pricing

**Scores:** Demand 8 | Competition 5 | Buildability 9 | Revenue Speed 9

**Evidence:** Developers report token count mismatches causing budget surprises; no standardized tool exists to normalize token counts across providers; cost estimation remains manual and error-prone in multi-provider setups

**Why no competition:** Requires maintaining accurate token counting for each provider separately and building a unified comparison layer—too granular for cost management platforms, too provider-specific for general tools

---

## #3 — AI Fine-Tuning ROI & Performance Degradation Monitor (Score: 30/40)

**Problem:** Companies invest in fine-tuning custom LLMs but have no way to measure whether the fine-tuned model actually performs better than the base model in production, or when fine-tuned models degrade over time

**Scores:** Demand 7 | Competition 7 | Buildability 9 | Revenue Speed 7

**Evidence:** Reddit r/LocalLLMs and r/OpenAI users report fine-tuning experiments that show gains in lab but fail in production; enterprises mention wasted budgets on fine-tuning with no measurable improvement; no product benchmarks fine-tuned vs base models automatically

**Why no competition:** Requires live production data collection and statistical analysis of model outputs—too specialized for generic ML monitoring platforms, requires domain knowledge of fine-tuning workflows
