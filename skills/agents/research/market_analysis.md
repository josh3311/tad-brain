# Skill: market_analysis

## Purpose
Analyze a market opportunity and score its profitability for Joshua.

## Instructions
1. Search for current market size and growth rate
2. Find 3-5 real competitors with revenue data if available
3. Identify the specific gap or opportunity
4. Score the opportunity 1-10 with clear reasoning
5. Write recommended action specifically for Joshua
6. Save full report to workflows folder

## Tools needed
- web_search
- file_write

## Output format
Save as workflows/market-analysis-[topic]-[date].md
Structure: Executive Summary, Opportunity Score, Top Competitors, Why Now, Action for Joshua, Sources

## Success criteria
Report is saved to file. Summary is under 5 sentences. Score has clear reasoning.
