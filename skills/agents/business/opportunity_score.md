# Skill: opportunity_score

## Purpose
Score a business idea or market gap for Joshua's business OS.

## Instructions
1. Search for proof this problem exists and people pay to solve it
2. Find existing solutions and their weaknesses
3. Estimate addressable market size
4. Score on 4 dimensions: Market Size, Competition Level, Ease of Entry, Profit Margin
5. Give overall score 1-10
6. Give Joshua one clear next action

## Tools needed
- web_search
- file_write

## Output format
Save as workflows/opportunity-[topic]-[date].md
Structure: Problem Proof, Existing Solutions, Market Size, Score Card, Verdict, Next Action

## Success criteria
Score card has all 4 dimensions. Next action is specific not generic.
